Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–7 working days. Express delivery is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Can I return an item?

Yes — we accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.

Do you offer assembly?

Yes, we offer a professional assembly service for an additional fee. You can add it at checkout.

Do you ship outside Egypt?

Currently we only deliver within Egypt. We are working on expanding internationally soon.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships you will receive a tracking number by email. You can also check your orders in your profile.

Can I customise a product?

Some items are available in custom sizes or colours. Contact us directly and we will do our best to help.
